Official sources of data

The following documents are excerpts from the Bulletin Officiel du CNRS.

The status of the CNRS personnel is regulated by the Circulaire n° CIR130864DRH, originally issued on 12 March 2013 and, since then, updated many times. This document implements in practice, at the CNRS level, the French law n° 2020-1674 on public research.

One of the annexes of this bulletin is the barème de rémunération (salary table) of the CNRS contract personnel. This table and its updated versions are the sources of the salary tables reported here. The updates, decided by the CNRS RH management, are listed below by implementation date:

  • DEC200245DRH

  • DEC202364DRH

  • DEC212574DRH

  • DEC221342DRH (repealed by the following)

  • DEC221962DRH & DEC221961DRH

  • DEC234313DRH

  • DEC234716DRH
